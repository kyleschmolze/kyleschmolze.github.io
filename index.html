About me

I'm a software engineer and entrepreneur living in Berkeley, CA, currently working on my second company, Groupmuse, which I love dearly and won't be leaving any time soon.

Sometimes I dodge the words "entrepreneur" and "startup" due to my exposure to some of the more toxic elements of American startup culture. However, that's silly, because I do believe that the internet is ultimately a democratizing force, and I believe that technology can make the lives of humans longer, stronger, happier, and healthier. (Although it remains to be shown that the benefits will be well-distributed. Let's work on that!)

I'm always on the search for new initiatives that combine technology and social good, in the form of social enterprises, B-corps, worker cooperatives, or anything else! If you're involved in one, I'd love to hear about it.

You can also find me at groupmuses in the Bay Area, or playing ultimate with Classy.
